How Basketball Started

Basketball was invented in 1891 by James Naismith in Massachusetts.

He used a soccer ball and peach baskets for the first game.

Important Moments

  • 1891 - Basketball invented
  • 1936 - Basketball becomes an Olympic sport
  • 1946 - NBA founded

Timeline

1891 - First game
1979 - 3-point line introduced
1980s - Basketball goes global
